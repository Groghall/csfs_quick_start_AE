Module 6 - Compliance Mid-Month (opt in)

What you'll need:

    ➢ Bulk opt in file

Summary

Within this module we will demonstrate how to bulk opt in/join employees mid pay reference period (PRP)

During the month Lisa Porter and Neil Brundish contacted ABC Employer and confirmed that they wished to opt in.


AEWS uses the payroll close date to determine which PRP the employee should be assessed to ensure that they still meet the criteria to opt in.

AEWS assumes that an employee who is either an eligible or non eligible jobholder with contributions below the regulatory minimum should join the scheme.

Internal Steps ABC Employer took

➢ Recorded the date the opt in notice was received from the employees (effective date)

➢ Completed the bulk opt in file

➢ Uploaded the .csv file to AEWS mid PRP

➢ Did not update their payroll existing member indicator (unless they joined the scheme)

Steps for you to complete
  1. Select ‘Employee Management’ from the bottom navigation bar

  2. Select ‘Start’ next to ‘Opt in/join’

  3. Select ‘Managers’

  4. Upload the bulk opt in file

  5. Note the enrolment date of the employees (assessment date) and the PRP this falls in

  6. Select ‘Confirm’

We have now successfully processed the opt in for these employees, AEWS will now assess these employees in the correct PRP to ensure they still meet the criteria. AEWS will confirm whether the opt in has been confirmed and produce the relevant communication.

Key learning outcome

➢ Completing the bulk opt in process does not opt that employee into the current PRP, AEWS follows the regulations and diaries the employee for assessment in the relevant PRP.

➢ Do not update the existing member indicator unless the employee has either joined or the Opt in has been confirmed by AEWS
