Module 3 - Certification

What you'll need:

    ➢ ABC Employer Fact Find

Summary

Within this module we will certify the contribution basis for the groups that we have set up. There are 4 different types of contribution basis that an employer can certify on but for ABC Employer they have selected 9% Pensionable and 7% Total.

AEWS monitors pension contributions against the regulatory minimums of the chosen certification basis, as well as any scheme level minimums that you may have selected.

For our case study the Directors and Management group are certified on 9% Pensionable but will be paying 5% employer and 5% employee contributions.

Internal steps ABC Employer took

➢ As part of their AE strategy they have chosen the certification basis for each distinct group of employees

➢ Agreed the length of time that they would certify on that basis

Steps for you to complete
  1. Log into AEWS test site/ continue if still logged in to step 2

  2. Select 'Start Self-Certification' then 'Model & Certify'

  3. Select 'Yes' as indicated in the image to use the file uploaded in module 2, then click 'Continue'
