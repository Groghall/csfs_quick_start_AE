Module 3 - Certification

Steps for you to complete continued
  1. Select 'Modelling/Certification' and ensure that the certification starting period starts from the staging date e.g. 1st April 2015



  2. Certify both groups on the basis as stated within the fact find. and subit certification

  3. You can view the certificates under the 'edit' button and check that the details are correct



  4. Download and save the certificates to the AEWS Folder

We have now successfully certified the contribution basis for the two groups that we have set up.
