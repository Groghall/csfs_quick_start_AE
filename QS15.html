Module 9 - Settings

Summary

Now you’ve completed all the modules we recommend going into the settings tab and seeing how you can configure AEWS to your company needs


Within settings you can configure communications and edit email content.

Configure Communications

➢ Company Branding - allows you add headers, footers and to ability to edit the text colour of your communications

➢ Letter Variables - allows you to amend the contact information, add scanned signatures and edit the Salary Sacrifice reference your communications

➢ Edit Attachments - allows you to upload attachments that need to be electronically sent with your worker communications (there is a size limit)

Edit email content

Within this section you can:


➢ Edit your electronic covering employee communications

➢ Add the attachments you uploaded in ‘Configure Communications’.


All of this can be applied at group or employer level.
