Client background

ABC Employer is a small re-cycling firm that has been running for 5 years.

The company directors have never provided their employees with a pension scheme; however because of auto enrolment they were aware of their duty to provide a 'qualifying pension scheme for their employees'.

Client Set-Up

ABC Employer has the following set up for their company:

  • 2 Company directors
  • 5 managers
  • 10 Warehouse staff
Preparation for Auto-Enrolment

18 months prior to their staging date ABC Employers started assessing their entire workforce, documenting internal process and agreeing their AE strategy. Their strategy consisted of:

    ➢ 2 groups, one for the directors and managers and another group for the warehouse staff

    ➢ As the warehouse has a high turnover of staff with variable earnings they decided to apply a 2 month postponement to those employees

    ➢ Every employee is paid on a monthly basis with a mixture of contractual (salary) and non contractual (overtime) earnings

    ➢ For certification the Director and Management group were based on 9% pensionable earnings and the Warehouse staff were based on 7% total pay. They have decided to use phased tiering for their contributions
