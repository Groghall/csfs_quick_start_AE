Welcome to the Auto Enrol Workplace Solution Quick Start Guide

Auto-Enrol Workplace Solution AEWS has been designed to be flexible to the differing needs that employers may face whilst tackling auto-enrolment (AE). The system is simple to use but does require an understanding of AE.

AEWS has been designed to help you manage your AE obligations. The responsibility for complying with the regulations will ultimately be yours as an employer. The system will help you but you should expect to have to work closely with your payroll, HR and pension administration teams to make sure that you are compliant.

This guide has been designed to be a simple step by step guide to AEWS, highlighting the various journeys and detailing how the system should form part of your regular process. It can be used in conjunction with the AEWS user guide.
